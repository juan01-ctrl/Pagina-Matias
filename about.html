About Dr. Verna

Dr Verna is a psychiatrist in Manhattan who specializes in child and adolescent psychiatry. He is board certified in adult psychiatry as well as in child and adolescent psychiatry. He has extensive experiencemanaging a wide range of issues and disorders working both in psychotherapy and in medication management. Dr Verna received a BA from The George Washington University in Washington DC and an MD from Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. He completed an Internship in Medicine and Residency in Psychiatry and proceeded to a Fellowship in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry at the Mount Sinai School of Medicine in New York.
He opened a private practice in 2008 after completing training.
